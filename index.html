Article II

Membership

FPG is open to all adult men and women of any age and skill level. Membership is individual and non-transferable — your spot is yours alone.

🏌🏾
Get a GHIN Handicap
You must obtain and maintain a USGA handicap index (GHIN). This keeps games fair for everyone and is required to participate in weekly competitions.
📋
Complete 3 Rounds
New members must play three official rounds with the group to establish their baseline and earn full active membership status.
📅
Stay Active
Play at least one official round every 5 weeks to maintain active status. Miss that window and you may be moved to the inactive roster — though exceptions exist for medical or personal hardship.
📆
Annual Renewal
Membership is valid for a calendar year (Jan–Dec) and rolls over automatically unless you withdraw by December 31st. Dues are due by January 31st each year.
Your Member Benefits: Discounted group golf rates · Weekly prize competitions · Monthly & annual awards · Eligibility to run for leadership positions · Access to group trips and special events

Bringing a Guest?

  • ✓Guests are welcome at management's discretion — invite them as a member in good standing.
  • ✓Guests must have an active USGA GHIN handicap to participate in weekly games.
  • ✗Guests are not eligible for awards, prizes, or voting privileges.
  • ✓All guests must comply with FPG rules, USGA guidelines, and course policies.
Article VI

How We Play

FPG runs five standard competitions every round. Here's what you're playing for each week.

Game How It Works Who Can Win
🏆 Champion of the Day $5 buy-in Best overall performance for the round. The top scorer takes the title and the pot. All active members
📊 Quota / Stableford $5 buy-in Modified Stableford scoring system. Points awarded per hole based on your handicap quota. Beat your number, earn points. All active members
💰 Skins $10 buy-in Win a hole outright (lowest net score) and you take the skin. Ties carry over to the next hole. All active members
🎯 Closest to the Pin (CTP) $5 buy-in Designated par-3 holes. Closest tee shot to the pin wins the pot. All active members
🎲 Blind Draw $5 buy-in Each participant draws a poker chip — the name drawn wins $10. When $5 is left over, that remainder becomes the Blind Squirrel pot ($5 winner). All active members
Scoring System: FPG uses the Modified Stableford scoring system for all gaming, per Article VI, Section 6.2. USGA rules and local course rules apply at all times. Scores are recorded through the FPG Scoring Application (official app, effective July 2026).

How Your Money Is Managed

  • 🏦All FPG funds are held in an FDIC-insured bank account with two authorized signatories.
  • 📊Quarterly financial statements are provided to all members — you always know where the money goes.
  • 🔍An annual audit is conducted by a three-member review committee appointed by the General Manager.
  • 📋App cost payments are itemized in all treasury reports for full transparency.
Article VII

Code of Conduct

FPG is built on respect — for each other, for course staff, and for the game. Here's what's expected.

🤝
Respect Everyone
Conduct yourself professionally toward fellow members, guests, and all course staff at all times.
⛳
Play by the Rules
USGA rules, local course rules, and all FPG policies apply every round, no exceptions.
🗣️
Handle Disputes Properly
Any disputes with a member, guest, or course staff go to the General Manager in writing — not on the course.
🔒
Protect the Group's Reputation
Your behavior on and off the course reflects on FPG. Conduct that harms the group's standing will be addressed.
Grievance Process: If you're disciplined by the management team and disagree with the decision, you have 14 days to appeal in writing. A three-member appeals committee (appointed by the GM, excluding all parties involved) will issue a final decision within 30 days.

Quick Reference

New Member FAQ

  • Q
    When do I need to pay dues?
    FY2027 annual dues of $122 are due by January 31, 2027. The 2026 app assessment of $36 is due by July 31, 2026.
  • Q
    What if I miss 5 weeks and go inactive?
    Email the management committee to request reinstatement. You'll need to play one official round within the next 5 weeks after approval to regain active status.
  • Q
    Can I bring someone to play with the group?
    Yes — guests are welcome with management approval. They must have a GHIN handicap but are not eligible for prizes or voting.
  • Q
    How do I know my Stableford quota?
    Your quota is set by your GHIN handicap index. The FPG Scoring Application calculates this automatically each round.
  • Q
    How do I get paid my winnings?
    Payouts are distributed after each round via the group's standard payment channels (Zelle, Cash App, etc.).
  • Q
    Where do I find round schedules and sign-ups?
    All FPG communications go through the group WhatsApp. Make sure you're connected — that's where sign-ups, results, and announcements are posted.
  • Q
    Who do I contact with questions?
    Reach out to Sting Ray (Ray Elmore) directly via WhatsApp for anything membership, dues, or operations related.
